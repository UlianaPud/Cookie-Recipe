Sprinkles Cookie Recipe

  • Time: 10min
  • Serves: 10 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 1/3 cup
Softened Butter 1 stick + 2 tbsp
Flour 1 cup
Sprinkles 2 grams

Step 1: Pour the amount of sugar into a large bowl.

Step 2: Add the butter(softened) in the bowl and mix it with an electric mixer.

Step 3 When the two ingredients are mixed togther in a soft consistance, now add flour andmix it again until cobiend.

Step 4 When it's mixed, you can use your hands to form a a piece of dough looking like a doughnut, place it on a lined cookie sheet.

Step 5 Put them in the oven and bake them at 325 feronyite for 15 minutes.

Final Step Now you can add your favourite sprinkles on top of cookie/doughnout
